About Us


MMU Marketplace is an online platform designed to make it convenient for MMU students to buy and sell products within the MMU campus community. Our mission is to create a safe, convenient, and student-friendly marketplace where students can connect, trade, and support each other. Our website also provides a user-friendly interface, a secure login system, a campus-focused community, and affordable deals for students.
